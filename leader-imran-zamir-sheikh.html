Imran Zamir Sheikh

Managing Partner | Assurance Corp - Auditors & Advisers | 30+ Years Experience | Middle East & Pakistan

IZS 30+ Years Experience Middle East & Pakistan

Imran Zamir Sheikh boasts over three decades of experience in the audit, corporate finance, and business advisory fields, with a focus on the Middle East and Pakistan. He began his career at Ernst & Young in 1988 and earned his Chartered Accountant qualification in 1996.

Throughout his career, Imran has worked with businesses of all sizes across industries including oil and gas, textiles, construction, and trading. He has conducted valuation assignments for the Privatization Commission and contributed to World Bank assignments related to the transport and power sectors.
